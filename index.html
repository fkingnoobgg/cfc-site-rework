Ignite Mentoring Pairer

Volunteer Tutor Exchange

A website built on Angular JS to pair experienced tutors with students who need help. This site was created in partnership with UWA guild volunteering so tutors also get volunteering hours added to academic transcipt.

Guild Volunteering Logbooks

An alternative to Guild Volunteering's physical logbooks. By making logbooks electronic it allows for easier access, searching and storage, making everyone's experience more pleasent when dealing with the logbooks.
